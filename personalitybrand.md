# Personality Brand Movie Theme

## Objective

Build a cinematic dark mode portfolio site that feels like a personal internet home, not a generic software engineer portfolio.

## Core Idea

The site should feel like the movie of my life: calm, personal, modern, and interactive.

It is inspired by the ideas:

- "be the hero of your own movie"
- "if you can dream it, you can do it"

The portfolio should feel less like a resume and more like entering a digital world built around software, storytelling, community, ambition, and curiosity.

## Design Direction

The design should use dark mode as the default atmosphere, with clean typography and a restrained cinematic layout.

Visual direction:

- Dark mode foundation
- Clean, modern typography
- Subtle motion
- Simple navigation
- Electric blue accents
- Emerald accents
- Subtle gold highlights
- Minimal visual noise
- Spacious layouts that feel intentional

The site should feel polished and personal without becoming flashy or generic.

## Stack

- Astro
- Vercel
- Supabase optional for backend or content later

## Interactivity

Add simple interactive elements that make the site feel alive without overwhelming the calm cinematic mood.

Suggested interactive elements:

- Hover effects on navigation and section cards
- Animated page transitions
- Command-style navigation
- Timeline cards
- Featured story cards
- Subtle active states
- Interactive homepage cards
- Motion that respects `prefers-reduced-motion`

## Site Experience

The homepage should act like the opening scene of the movie.

It should immediately establish:

- Who Itay is
- Where he is coming from and where he is now
- What worlds visitors can enter
- Why the site feels personal instead of corporate

Each section should feel like a different scene, chapter, or room in the digital world.

The pages can start simple and become richer over time as more context, stories, links, interviews, and projects are added.

## Goal

Make visitors feel like they entered the digital world of a software developer, storyteller, creator, and community builder.

The final experience should feel like a personal movie interface: calm, cinematic, interactive, and unmistakably Itay.
